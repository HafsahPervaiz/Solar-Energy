Current Solar Policies

Various policies support the growth of solar energy, including:

  • Investment Tax Credits (ITC)
  • Renewable Portfolio Standards (RPS)
  • Net Metering
  • Solar Renewable Energy Certificates (SRECs)

Advocacy Efforts

Our advocacy efforts focus on:

  • Promoting favorable solar policies at local, state, and federal levels
  • Educating policymakers about the benefits of solar energy
  • Collaborating with environmental organizations to push for clean energy initiatives
  • Engaging communities in solar energy discussions and decision-making processes

Get Involved in Solar Policy

You can make a difference by:

  • Contacting your local representatives to express support for solar-friendly policies
  • Participating in public hearings and town halls on energy issues
  • Joining or supporting solar advocacy groups
  • Staying informed about current solar policies and proposed changes
