What is Solar Energy?

Solar energy is the radiant light and heat from the Sun that is harnessed using a range of technologies such as solar heating, photovoltaics, solar thermal energy, solar architecture, molten salt power plants and artificial photosynthesis.

How Solar Energy Works

Solar panels convert sunlight into electrical energy through the photovoltaic effect. When photons from sunlight hit the solar cells, they knock electrons free from atoms, generating a flow of electricity.

Benefits of Solar Energy

  • Renewable and clean energy source
  • Reduces electricity costs
  • Low maintenance costs
  • Technological advancements are constantly improving efficiency
  • Contributes to energy independence
