Frequently Asked Questions

What is the impact of solar energy on climate change?
Solar energy significantly reduces greenhouse gas emissions by replacing fossil fuel-based electricity generation. This helps mitigate climate change by lowering the overall carbon footprint of our energy consumption.
How affordable is solar energy for individuals?
Solar energy has become increasingly affordable due to technological advancements and government incentives. Many homeowners can now install solar panels with little to no upfront costs through leasing options or power purchase agreements. Additionally, the long-term savings on electricity bills often outweigh the initial investment.
What are the main benefits of switching to solar energy?
The main benefits of switching to solar energy include reduced electricity costs, increased energy independence, lower carbon footprint, potential increase in property value, and contribution to a cleaner environment. Solar energy also creates jobs in the renewable energy sector and can provide energy security in remote areas.
